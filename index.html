Imran Hossain

Quantifying narrative. Qualifying data.

I am a Research Associate at the BRAC Institute of Governance and Development (BIGD), BRAC University, where I am part of the Gender and Social Development Cluster, working under the supervision of Dr. Shaila Ahmed. My work involves data collection, quantitative analysis, and applied policy research — building hands-on experience in development-focused empirical work.

Alongside my professional work, I am pursuing an MS in Mathematics at Ahsanullah University of Science and Technology. Before that, I completed my BSc in Economics at Jahangirnagar University.

Prior to joining BIGD, I worked full-time for over a year as a feature journalist at The Business Standard, one of Bangladesh's leading English-language dailies, covering over a hundred stories on socioeconomic issues, culture, education, and technology.

I aim to pursue higher studies in economics. My academic interests lie in microeconomics, political economy, and development economics.
